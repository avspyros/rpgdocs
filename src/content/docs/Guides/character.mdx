import { Steps, Tabs, TabItem, Badge } from '@astrojs/starlight/components';

### Follow these steps to quickly create a character:

<Steps>

1. **Roll Ability Scores**

   <Tabs>
     <TabItem label="Roll 3d6 for each ability">
       **Charisma, Constitution, Dexterity, Intelligence, Strength, Wisdom,** in order, and record them on your
       character sheet. A player can decide to swap the position of one ability score that was rolled with another.
     </TabItem>
     <TabItem label="Note modifiers">
       Each ability score has a [Modifier](../reference/abilities) which is associated with it.
     </TabItem>
     <TabItem label="Is the Character suitable?">
       If the total of all ability score modifiers is less than 0, discard and start again.
     </TabItem>
   </Tabs>

2. **Choose a Character Class**

   <Tabs>
     <TabItem label="Classes">
       <Badge text="Cleric" size="small" variant="note" />
       <Badge text="Fighter" size="small" />
       <Badge text="Magic User" size="small" variant="tip" />
       <Badge text="Specialist" size="small" variant="caution" />
       <Badge text="Dwarf" size="small" />
       <Badge text="Elf" size="small" variant="success" />
       <Badge text="Halfling" size="small" variant="note" />
     </TabItem>
     <TabItem label="Determine Hit Points">
       1st level characters have a minimum number of HP according to their class. If the rolled amount is less than the
       indicated number on this [chart], simply use the number shown on the chart instead. Always add the Constitution
       modifier to the result.
     </TabItem>
     <TabItem label="Record modifiers & Saving Throws">
       Record To-Hit Bonuses and Saving Throws on the Character sheet.
     </TabItem>
   </Tabs>

3. **Select Alignment**

       conditions in the starting area of play. Apart from that, they can use the cheapest column prices from the
       equipment table to buy their starting equipment.
     </TabItem>
   </Tabs>

5. **Choose a Name**

   Anything not (that) ridiculous...

</Steps>
