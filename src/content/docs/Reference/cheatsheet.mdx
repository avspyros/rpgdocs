<Aside title="Actor:" type="caution">
  Character, PC, NPC or creature.
</Aside>

<CardGrid>
  <Card title="Surprise">
    - Roll a d6 for each side which might be surprised
      - **1–2** → surprised → cannot act this round
      - **3–6** → not surprised → can act this round
    - Ambushers if they exist roll a d6:
      - **1–4** → able to act on surprise round
      - **5–6** → fail to act on surprise round
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sneak Attack">
    - Attacking surprised (or unaware) actors.
    - For specialists with at least 2 points in the skill:
      - +2 attack bonus.
      - Multiply damage by number of Sneak points.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Attacking unaware foe from behind">
    - **Foe:** loses Dex & shield AC bonuses.
    - **Attacker:** +2 extra Attack bonus.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Parry">
    - Decide to parry at any point during the Round.
    - No other action is allowed.
    - +2 AC for the Round.
    - +4 AC for Elves & Dwarves.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Charge">
    - Use Running Move.  
    - x2 damage.
    - -2 AC penalty.
  
  </Card>
  <Card title="Press">
    - Only Fighters, Elves & Dwarves.
    - +2 attack.
    - -4 AC.

  </Card>
  <Card title="Defensive Fighting">
    - Only Fighters, Elves & Dwarves.
    - +2 AC.
    - -4 attack.

  </Card>
    <Card title="Casting Spells">
    - Caster needs both hands free 
    - Exceptions (on one hand) when casting a spell: 
      - Staff (Magic-User) 
      - Holy symbol (Cleric) 
    - Cannot move at all during the round of the spellcasting
    - Cannot cast if taken damage earlier in the Round

  </Card>
</CardGrid>
